About Us

T'sarE Llc is an entertainment company working on it's first big video game, Galaxy Gang. Our team thrives on developing the skills required to make memorable entertainment for game enthusiasts both young and old.

Mission

To create influential entertainment that provokes all to develop skills, share ideas, and accept one another to better collaborate.

Vision

To build a collective of like minded artists that spread the word of equality through fun.

Promise

We will expand our knowledge to improve the games that encourage all of us to become better humans.

Passion

The drive that inspires and pushes us to think beyond the boundaries of what is possible.
